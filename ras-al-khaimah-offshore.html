Ras Al Khaimah Offshore

RAK International Corporate Center (RAKICC) is a corporate registration entity in Ras Al Khaimah, one of the emirates of the United Arab Emirates. RAKICC is the only entity that assists with setting up offshore companies and international businesses in the region. RAKICC is highly ranked among offshore registries worldwide and offers a range of services for business operations.

RAKICC helps businesses with setting up international entities, provides asset protection, offers tax planning, and facilitates the setup of corporate bank accounts in the UAE.
