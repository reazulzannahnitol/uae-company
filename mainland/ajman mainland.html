Ajman Mainland

Your gateway to business success in Ajman.

Ajman is an emirate where a variety of businesses such as manufacturing, farming, and fishing exist. With its new construction of beautiful beach sides and resorts, the demand for restaurants and luxurious lifestyles has been thriving. Having a business setup in Ajman means starting with an LLC legal entity, which is the first step for a company formation in Ajman, requiring at least two people to start a business. Like every other emirate in the UAE, you will need a UAE national as a local partner. According to Ajman’s business laws, the local sponsor will have a 51% share of the company, while the foreign investor or owner will receive the remaining 49%.

For an Ajman company setup, give us a call and we will guide you through all the important steps, necessary paperwork, and help determine what license best suits your company.

How to Get Ajman Mainland License?

For a company setup in Ajman mainland, the process must be fulfilled by the Department of Economic Development in the Emirate. Once this is done to set up the business and the jurisdiction is finalized, you will get the business license under the given jurisdiction.

Benefits of Business Setup in Ajman Mainland

  • 100% foreign ownership
  • 100% legal exemption from import and export duties
  • Low rental costs and business setup costs
  • Lower land prices
  • Lower living expenses

Types of Business Licenses in Ajman Mainland

  • Trading License
  • Professional Trading
  • National Industrial License
  • Industrial License
  • E-Commerce License
  • General Maintenance License

Types of Legal Entities for Your Company in Ajman Mainland

  • Joint Liability Company
    A company or business shared between two partners who must be from the UAE.
  • Simple Commanding Company
    A company structure that can be shared between more than two partners. All partners must be UAE locals, with a share not less than 51%.
  • Limited Liability Company
    Businesses can be owned by investors or shareholders up to a maximum of 50 people. The partners aren’t liable for the company’s liability beyond their shares in the capital.
  • Service Agents
    The investor or national must be from Ajman. The owner of the license will be required to meet all liabilities pertaining to the establishment towards others.
