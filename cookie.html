So, let's talk about cookies. Not the edible kind, but digital cookies and similar technologies like tags, web beacons, pixels, and SDKs for mobile apps. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites to improve your browsing experience. We use various types of cookies and similar technologies, like web beacons and pixels, to provide, secure, and improve our services. These help us understand how you interact with our website and ensure a smooth experience.

Types of Cookies We Use

When you visit our website and consent to cookies, these are the types of cookies that may be downloaded to your device:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for the website's core functionality, ensuring smooth administration and operation of the website and app.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences to deliver a more personalized experience, displaying content relevant to you.
  • Analytical Cookies: Help us analyze website usage for improvements, such as counting visitors, session durations, and navigation patterns.
  • Advertising Cookies: Used for targeted advertising and cross-site tracking, remembering your visit, the pages viewed, and any links followed to other websites.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You’re in control of your cookies. You can adjust your preferences through the cookie banner on our website or through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain features of our site.

Until you make up your mind, we'll only use the strictly necessary cookies.

To adjust your cookie settings, you can use the options on the banner that appears on our website or through your browser settings.

For more guidance on managing cookies, visit aboutcookies.org or allaboutcookies.org.

Please note, if you decide to go cookie-free (including the strictly necessary ones), some features of our website may not be accessible.
